How to separate egg

Separate the egg yolk and the egg white. In a bowl, beat the 7 egg yolks

Step 1
How to prepare meringue

Pour the egg white in a mixing bowl then add 1/2tsp cream of tartar.Beat using an electric mixer until foamy. Then add gradually 3/4 cup of white sugar. Beat over high speed until fluffy and stiff peaks form

Step 2
How to prepare for the final baking

Then gradually add the meringue into your cake batter and mix slowly using a spatula. Transfer the mixture into a baking pan with parchment paper. Tap 3 times to remove the bubbles.

Step 3
Baking
